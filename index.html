Immersive VR Experience

Welcome to your immersive journey!
Please click **"Start Experience"** to begin. If you're on a mobile device and want to use your phone's movement to look around, you may also need to click **"Enable Gyroscope"** to grant permission.
IMPORTANT: This player requires a direct MP4 link that supports CORS for 360 VR playback. Hosting videos directly on GitHub rarely works for this.

Once started, move your phone around or drag your mouse to look around in the video.
